Reverse Logistics Made Simple

Returns. Recycling. Redistribution.

What is Reloop?

Reloop is a reverse logistics platform that simplifies the way businesses and customers manage returns, recycling, and redistribution of products. We enable circular commerce by closing the loop efficiently and sustainably.

Our Mission

To empower a sustainable future by making reverse logistics effortless, smart and accessible to everyone.

Our Vission

To be the go-to platform for managing product returns and recycling across Africa and beyond - enabling a circular economy for generations to come.

Our Services

  • Return management for eCommerce businesses
  • Eco-friendly product recycling coordination
  • Reditsribution and restocking of returned goods
  • Reverse logistics analytics and optimization
